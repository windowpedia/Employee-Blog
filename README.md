Employee Blog is a little project I made for fun where you can make your own fake forum on practically anything you want to. It is pretty easy to use.

# htf do i use this?
<img width="1586" height="893" alt="Image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dae27fa6-ebdf-4852-a1a7-44d25c80bb7e" />
In the "Create Post" panel, enter an username of your choice, a fake email, and your message title and body. After that, the flair is what your "position" in the job is. (being that this is a forum for a job simulator, but you can use it for any reason.). Choosing "Employee" will make your username green. Choosing "Engineer" will make your username blue, choosing "CCO" will make your username red. Choosing "COO" will make your username black, choosing "President" will make your usernamme purple, and choosing "CEO" will make your username gold.

The "Upload Date" is the date the post was canonically set in. You can choose whenever you want to. Very easy to use. The "Rank" dropdown is a little Geometry Dash reference. Choosing "Featured" will have a yellow glow over your message. Choosing "Legendary" will make it purple. Choosing "Godlike" will make it a strong Cyan glow. You can also apply this by clicking the "Manage" button on an existing Post, in which you can pin and unpin messages too.
